WHY NSI

we're supporting
the war fighter

Naval Systems, Inc. (NSI), is a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business headquartered in Lexington Park, Maryland that primarily supports the Department of the Navy with operations at Patuxent River, MD; Cherry Point, NC; Virginia Beach, VA; Jacksonville, FL; San Diego, CA; China Lake, CA; Hunstville, AL; and Dayton, OH. NSI has developed a reputation among clients for superior analytics, innovation, and excellence in systems acquisition and life cycle sustainment support. NSI is mission-focused, people-centric, and operationally efficient.
